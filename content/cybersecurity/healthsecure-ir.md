## Scenario

HealthSecure Systems, a healthcare technology provider responsible for managing sensitive patient information, detected unusual activity within its network environment. Security analysts observed suspicious authentication events, network anomalies, and alerts generated by intrusion detection systems, raising concerns about a potential compromise.

As part of the **Incident Response team**, I was tasked with investigating the suspected security incident. Multiple evidence sources were provided, including Windows Event Logs, Zeek network records, Snort IDS alerts, phishing email artifacts, and forensic findings. My objective was to determine how the attack occurred, identify affected systems, reconstruct the attack timeline, and recommend containment and recovery actions.

The investigation followed a structured incident response process involving evidence collection, analysis, correlation, timeline reconstruction, root cause identification, and reporting. The final deliverable included a comprehensive incident report outlining findings, impact assessment, and remediation recommendations.
